    Overview

    The major trends right now are growth in AI/machine learning, 5G communications, Iot, data centers/cloud computing, automotive electronics, and advanced packaging. Below are major areas and players in the semiconductor industry.

    Semiconductor Equipment

    Tools and machines for chip manufacturing.

    • ASML - The dominant supplier of lithography systems for chip manufacturing.

    • Applied Materials - The world's largest semiconductor equipment company, broad portfolio of manufacturing tools.

    • Lam Research - Leading supplier of wafer fabrication equipment and services.

    • KLA Corporation - Manufactures inspection and metrology systems for managing chip yields.

    • Tokyo Electron - Major global supplier of semiconductor production equipment based in Japan.

    • Entegris - Provides advanced materials and solutions for semiconductor manufacturing.

    Foundries/Chip Manufacturing

    Fabrication plants that manufacture semiconductor chips.

    • TSMC - The world largest dedicated chip foundry, manufactures chips for many fabless companies.

    • Samsung - Major foundry player in addition to designing and manufacturing its own chips.

    • GlobalFoundries - Global pure-play foundry company, manufacturing chips for fabless firms.

    • UMC - Leading pure-play foundry based in Taiwan, caters to fabless chip designers.

    • Intel - Leading integrated device manufacturer that both designs and manufactures its own chips.

    Testing/Probing

    Equipment for testing semiconductor devices.

    • Teradyne - Leading supplier of automatic test equipment for semiconductors and electronic systems.

    • Advantest - Major manufacturer of test systems for memory, SoCs, and other semiconductor devices.

    • Cohu - Designs and manufactures semiconductor test handling equipment and interface solutions.

    • FormFactor - Develops advanced semiconductor wafer probe cards for chip testing.

    CPU/GPU/SoC Design Houses

    • Intel - The world's largest semiconductor chip maker, specializing in x86 CPUs for PCs and servers.

    • AMD - Intel's main competitor in x86 CPUs for PCs and servers, also makes GPUs.

    • Qualcomm - Leading fabless designer of mobile SoCs using ARM architecture.

    • Nvidia - Graphics and AI chipmaker

    • Broadcom - Major fabless chip supplier focused on wireless, networking, and infrastructure software.

    • Marvell - Fabless designer of storage, networking, wireless, and embedded processor chips.

    • AMD - Fabless designer of x86 CPUs and GPUs, competitor to Intel and Nvidia.

    • Apple - Fabless designer of custom ARM-based chips for iPhones and Macs.

    Memory Chips

    Chips that store data, including DRAM and flash memory.

    • Samsung - Major memory chip supplier, #1 in DRAM and NAND flash memory.

    • SK Hynix - Second largest DRAM supplier globally after Samsung.

    • Micron - Major DRAM and NAND flash memory manufacturer.

    • Toshiba - Major NAND flash memory supplier for storage and consumer devices.

    Analog/Mixed Signal

    Chips that process real-world analog signals.

    • Texas Instruments - Global leader in analog chips and embedded processors.

    • Analog Devices - Major analog chip company focused on high performance analog, mixed-signal, and DSP.

    • Maxim Integrated - Designs analog chips and sensors for automotive, industrial, healthcare applications. Part of Analog Devices now.

    • STMicroelectronics - European chipmaker that manufactures analog, mixed-signal, and MEMS chips.

    Microcontrollers

    Small computers-on-a-chip for controlling devices.

    • Microchip - Leading microcontroller supplier for embedded and IoT applications.

    • STMicroelectronics - Global semiconductor company that manufactures mainstream and automotive microcontrollers.

    • Renesas - Major microcontroller and embedded systems provider from Japan.

    • NXP Semiconductors - Key microcontroller provider for automotive, security, and IoT applications.

    • Texas Instruments - Massive microcontroller portfolio for industrial and embedded use cases.

    • Silicon Labs - Leading provider of wireless-enabled microcontrollers for IoT.

    Automotive and Power Semiconductors

    Chips built for automotive applications and power management.

    • Infineon - Leading automotive semiconductor supplier and power semiconductor manufacturer.

    • STMicroelectronics - Major provider of ICs and discrete power semiconductors for automotive and industrial applications.

    • On Semiconductor - Leading supplier of power management, analog, sensing, and connectivity chips for EVs and advanced driver-assistance systems (ADAS).

    • NXP Semiconductors - Significant automotive chip portfolio for ADAS, vehicle networking, powertrain and safety applications.

    • Renesas - Key automotive microcontroller and system-on-chip (SoC) supplier based in Japan.

    Here are overviews for the major semiconductor design software companies:

    EDA (Electronic Design Automation) Software

    Software for designing and verifying integrated circuits.

    • Synopsys - Leading EDA vendor, provides chip design and verification tools.

    • Cadence Design Systems - EDA and IP company, offers software for full chip design workflow.

    • Siemens EDA - EDA tools for IC and system design, including simulation and verification.

    Simulation and PCB

    • Ansys - Multiphysics simulation software used to design electronic components.

    • Altium - Leading PCB and electronic design automation software.

    Semiconductor IP

    Reusable chip design blocks.

    • ARM Holdings - Leading provider of semiconductor IP such as CPU and GPU cores. Owned by SoftBank.

    • Rambus - Semiconductor IP licensing company, known for memory and interface solutions.

    • Ceva - Licenses DSP cores and platforms for AI, IoT, 5G, and automotive applications.

    Here are overviews of key semiconductor materials companies:

    Materials

    Substrates, gases, silicon used to produce chips.

    • Sumco - Leading manufacturer of silicon wafers used to fabricate chips. Based in Japan.

    • Shin-Etsu - Major supplier of silicon wafers as well as rare earth magnets and semiconductor chemicals.

    • Corning - Produces specialized glass substrates, glasses, and ceramics for semiconductor fabs.

    • Air Liquide - Global industrial gas company that provides gases and chemicals for semiconductor manufacturing.

    • Air Products - Supplies nitrogen, oxygen, argon, helium, and gases worldwide for semiconductor production.
